Online appointment booking process
Register an account on the website

Register a new account if you don't have an account yet. You need to provide a valid username, password and email address to facilitate future contact.

Log in to your account on the website

Enter the website address and log in with your registered account and password

Register personal information

Open Your Profile page and provide all required information (Fields marked with red Asterisk)

Make an appointment

Select the time you want to make an appointment to see the list of doctors on duty. Click the Choose button on the doctor's information bar and edit the necessary information in the Medical Appointment Form if necessary to make an appointment.

Wait to receive appointment confirmation information from the doctor

Regularly check your email and appointment management page to see the doctor's form confirmation results

Bring medical appointment form for medical examination

Bring your medical appointment form and related documents to the hospital according to your appointment for medical examination

Make an appointment via hotline
Call the via hotline

Call the hospital's hotline to schedule an appointment at 0885 - xxx - xxx.

Provide personal information as instructed

Listen and follow operator instructions

Operator will guide you through the medical examination registration procedures

The operator will complete the appointment booking application and provide you with the necessary information

Additionally, you can make an appointment directly at the hospital

You can also go directly to the hospital to make an on-site appointment
